# Hey, I'm Javi 👋

**MuleSoft Integration Developer & AI Ethics Researcher**

I build enterprise integration architectures (Salesforce, NAV, OData, CloudHub 2.0) at Teknei/Eiffage by day, and research synthetic empathy and sycophancy in generative AI by night. Final-year CS student at UCLM (SICUE @ UPV/EHU, Donostia).

## What I'm working on

- 🔗 **Enterprise connectors** — Bidirectional Salesforce↔Dynamics NAV integrations, OAuth2 providers, API gateways with DataWeave & OpenAPI contracts
- 🧠 **TFG: Synthetic Empathy in Generative AI** — Exploring affective dependency, algorithmic paternalism, and the ethics of care in LLM interactions (Jonas, Bloom, EU AI Act)
- 📊 **Sycophancy persistence study** — Empirical 3×3 factorial design (Claude Sonnet, GPT-4o, Llama 3.3) measuring LLM compliance under factual refutation. Targeting EMNLP/EACL
- ✍️ Writing about AI ethics, philosophy of technology, and digital society at [javiergarciatercero.es](https://javiergarciatercero.es)
